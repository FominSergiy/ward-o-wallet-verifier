## Acceptance criteria

- Every `ServiceEvent` and `PhaseEvent` emitted from `src/agent/events.ts` carries `request_id: string` and `duration_ms: number`.
- `ServiceEvent` carries `cost_usd: number | null` (null when not paid).
- `LogEvent` is a discriminated union with explicit `level: "info" | "warn" | "error"` and `code?: string` instead of free-form strings.
- `request_id` is generated once at the route layer and propagated through `verify()`, `discover()`, `invokeAll()`, `synthesizeVerdict()`.
